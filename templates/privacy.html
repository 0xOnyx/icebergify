Privacy Policy

Icebergify was developed by Akshay Raj as an open source app powered by the Spotify Web API. This service is not intended for monetization. By choosing to use this app, you agree to the collection and use of information with relation to this policy. 

The information collected from the user is limited to the Spotify account ID, username, and top 50 tracks and artists over the past several years. None of the aforementioned data is used for any purpose apart from creating an "iceberg" ranking of users' commonly listened to artists. No information shared will be stored, used, or shared with any third parties.
